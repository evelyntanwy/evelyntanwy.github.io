Values and Passion

Respect, Responsibilities and Kindness

These values has always grew with me since I was little.

Over the years living in different countries and meeting different people has made me realised the importance of respecting others. As cliche as it sounds, it might not mean much to us, but would mean everything to someone else by treating them with respect.

Responsibilities is also a value I try to instill. We have to be responsible in accepting the path we made and to take charge on how we want them to form.

A little bit of kindness goes a long way. We do not know what others are going through. Just a simple gesture could change their life.

How my culture has influenced my values and identity

I would say growing up in Malaysia was wonderful. Malaysia is a multi racial country which consist of three major races, (Malay, Chinese and Indian). This has widely shape my values to understand the different culture of different families. I learn to be more accepting and respectful, by understanding the traditions and certain struggles of each different lifestyle. We have also a relatively laid back culture, which lead to a less stressful environment.

When we meet a friend we don't start by asking "How's your day?" instead we ask them "Have you eaten?" I guess due to it's diversity, we manage to find a common topic that could be relatable to everyone which is food. This had then cultivated into my life and only in recent years I have found my new love for cooking and baking. The feeling of preparing a wonderful meal and putting food on the table has made me feel blessed and thankful.

Describe your own strengths and limitations in relation to learning

My strengths would be diligence, when I put my heart into a certain task I will try to give my all to complete the task with my best efforts.

As for my limitations, I occasionally take a longer time to completed certain task or to grasp the concept of it.
