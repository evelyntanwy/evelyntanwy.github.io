About Me

Background

I was born and raised in Malaysia

I then left to Australia for studies and was living and working there for a number of years. Eventually decided to move back to Asia - Singapore to be closer to family and friends.

After working there for 2 years, I decided to embark on a new journey in New Zealand.

Just like that 4.5 years went by and I am extremely grateful to live in such a wonderful country and call New Zealand my home.

I am an Engineer by profession and had been working as a Systems Engineer maintaining Wellington trains before I decided to take a leap of faith to start this exciting journey with Enspiral Dev Academy (EDA).

Venturing into the tech space has made me realise the endless possibilities of creating and building things. Over the past couple of months I have cultivated the appreciation for coding when it dawned on me that I could automate a lot of the busy work in my life

Times when I'm not coding, you will most likely find me behind a sewing machine trying to making something. I find the simple joy of turning a large piece of fabric into a beautiful piece.
