About Me

Background

I was born and raised in Malaysia

I then left to Australia for studies and was living and working there for a number of years. Eventually decided to move back to Asia - Singapore to be closer to family and friends.

After working there for 2 years, I decided to embark on a new journey in New Zealand.

Just like that 4.5 years went by and I am extremely grateful to live in such a wonderful country and call New Zealand my home.

I am an Engineer by profession and had been working as a Systems Engineer maintaining Wellington trains before I decided to take a leap of faith to start this exciting journey with Dev Academy.
