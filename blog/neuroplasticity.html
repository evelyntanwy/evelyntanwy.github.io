Neuroplasticity

What is Neuroplasticity

Neuroplaticity is an ability for our brain to reorganize that form a new neural connections which occurs in our lifetime. It allows our nerve cells in the brain to redevelop from any injury or diseases that could help in the responses. Apart from that the responses to every new situations or to adapt in a change environment.

Neuroplaticity can occur by physically rewiring ourself, in which by changing our mindset though practices.

Growth Mindset

The meaning of growth mindset is celebrate trying, learn a new skill of learning, effort is a journey to a brighter life Growth mindset is finding joy in learning regardless of age, time and outcome. Of course we all want to succeed but sometime we get so caught up in how we want the end go to be and forget the process of it. Having growth mindset helps you celebrate trying, no matter the outcome you have try doing it and it is worth celebrating. We need to put in effort to build the journey in order for a brighter life.
