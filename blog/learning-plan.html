End of Foundations

What is your long term goal and/or career pathway?

Come out as a better and more competent web developer. I would like to combine both my skills (current and new - web development) and be able to help people in a way. As cliche as it sounds, I do wish my job involve something that is meaningful and could be a valuable asset. I would like to join a company that I share the same passion with. Also help me grow as a person and be able to use my skill wisely. Transitioning to the tech world, this can broaden the scope and not specifically in New Zealand but else where if needed.

What do you think your biggest strengths and limitations will be in Bootcamp?

I don’t think they have changed much, I mention my strengths is diligence, and I do see how it helped me pull though Foundations. At times when it got difficult I was diligent to read up more and practice more trying to solve the problem.

As for my limitations, based on the time boxing there were task that I did spend longer. But there was also task that I manage to spend less time with better understanding. I don’t think it would hinder my bootcamp journey, but to understand my limits and capabilities by working harder on topics that need more understanding.

What do you think your biggest non-technical challenge at Bootcamp will be?

Keeping focus. At times when you start looking into an issue, and tend to go down a rabbit hole looking into other issue and not solving the first issue. Taking it a step at a time and try to rectify them according.

What non-technical skills - human skills - would you like to see developed in yourself while at Dev Academy?

To improve my confidence with my work and self. Also be and be able to communicate with others well. This will def help with the atmosphere without any added stress.

What are your expectations from the Bootcamp team?

I hope the team will guide me in a right direction and to work on my weakness to better improve as a web developer. Helping me when self doubt comes in play and to assist me with a bit a push and hint when I’m not quite going the right way (in codes).

What are your expectations of yourself on Bootcamp

During the studying period to fully concentrate and not be distracted. Try as hard as I can and to be fully committed to coding and completing the task. Try to take some breaks even tho I have not solved the problem. Asking for help when needed or when google is not showing me the correct way.
