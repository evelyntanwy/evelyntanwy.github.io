HTML, CSS and Javascript

Think of it as a new build house on a section. HTML acts as an Engineer. They will need to understand the idea and concept of the website that is being build. They determine the structural integrity of the building. As for CSS it would be the Architect, that helps bring the idea to life and the vision of the house with colours and patterns. Javascript comes in to play as the Interior Designer which helps your make your house fancy, cosy and even smart. Fitting in different buttons and sections that needs bit of sprucing up.

Control flow, loops, array, objects

Control flow literal explanations would be controlling the flow, and in another word telling your computer the steps you want them to perform. If we go back to the idea of purchasing a furniture, the different section of the manual acts as the control flow, (eg, for top section please turn to page 12).

Loops acts as factor repeating the process on different section. As for the objects they represent the parts that puts the furniture in place such as the MDF board.

Array is the container that stores a number of items, such as the screw, wooden dowel and at times you might be lucky you get a mini allen key.
